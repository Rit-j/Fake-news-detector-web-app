About the App

The app is basically an ML model that uses natural language processing and then a series of diffrent algorithms to determine whether a news is real or fake. The app is not a fact-checker. Like humans, computer cannot analyze the text. Hence it has to be converted into some numerical values that the computer can interpret and work upon. First, the model performs stemming to omit the unnecessary words and then bring the rest of the words to their basic form. The count vectorizer then converts the text into some numerical values depending upon the frequency of the occuring word. It then applies Passive agressive algorithm over the vectorized text to generate features and then predict the result. The passive agressive algorithm is such that it compares the result to its prediction, if it is same, it becomes passive and does nothing. If it is not same, then it becomes aggressive and does the necessary changes to bring out the correct result.
